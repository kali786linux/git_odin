Coffee Dessert

This is a coffee dessert easy recipe

Ingredients

  • 100 grams ladyfingers (or Maria biscuits)
  • 50 grams almonds
  • 1 can condensed milk (cooked)
  • 500 mL milk
  • 5 eggs
  • 25 grams cornstarch
  • 1 Tbsp. sugar
  • 2 Tbsp. instant coffee

Method

  1. Grind the ladyfingers and almonds for a few seconds at speed 7/8/9.
  2. Set aside in a bowl.
  3. In the Thermomix cup add the milks, egg yolks, cornstarch and coffee. Set to 8 minutes/194Â°F/speed 3.

Enjoy!
