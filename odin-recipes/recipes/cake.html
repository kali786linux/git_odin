3 Min Chocolate Mug Cake

This is a 3 min cake thats fast and easy to make

Ingredients

  • 3tbsp cocoa powder
  • 4tbsp flour
  • 4tbsp sugar
  • 3tbsp oil
  • 3tbsp milk
  • 1 egg

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients together till mixture is thick
  2. bake in microwave for 3 min
  3. add extra sprinkles of your choice

Enjoy!
