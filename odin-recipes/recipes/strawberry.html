Iced Strawberry Dessert

This is an easy and tasty recipes

Ingredients

  • 250 grams strawberries (for souffle)
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • 3 egg whites
  • 200 grams sugar
  • 200 mL cream
  • 8 whole strawberries (for garnish, optional)

Method

  1. Line bowls with parchment paper up the sides, about 2 cm (approximately 3/4 inches).
  2. Mince strawberries, add lemon juice and set aside. In a bowl, beat egg whites until foamy.
  3. Heat bowl in a water bath, and continue to whisk while adding the sugar until stiff.

Enjoy!
